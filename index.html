about me

I made the leap into web development. Coming up with designs wasn’t enough. I had to build them too. Enrolling in DigitalCrafts Immersive Coding Bootcamp was a great move. I learned everything that was essential for a full stack web developer to know. And getting a chance to collaborate on projects was a great way to experience working with a group.

Hailing from Long Island, NY, I relocated to Atlanta, GA back in 2008. (Side note: I thought I was getting away from the snow, but it followed me here!) I have enjoyed exploring Georgia especially the vineyards. I’m a big wine enthusiast and a history buff. Grabbing a cup of coffee, catching up with friends and watching a movie are a few of my favorite things to do.

During my time at RentPath I’ve gone from graphic design, to the back end of sites with data feeds and to the front end with tagging. Adding in my new-found full stack web development skills really rounds out my knowledge of all things web. Not only can I design a website, I can build it from scratch and then report on it. Please check out some of my work below and feel free to contact me any time.
