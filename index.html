about me

I started my career in the creative department at RentPath (then Primedia) and have learned so much during my time at the company. My current role as Tag Analyst has opened the world of front end design and development. During my time with RentPath, I've gone from graphic design, to the back end of sites with data feeds, to the front end with tagging. Adding in my full stack web development skills from DigitalCrafts Immersive Coding Bootcamp really rounds out my knowledge of all things web. Not only can I design a website, I can build it from scratch and then report on it.

Hailing from Long Island, NY, I relocated to Atlanta, GA back in 2008. (Side note: I thought I was getting away from the snow, but it followed me here!) I have enjoyed exploring Georgia especially the vineyards. I'm a big wine enthusiast and a history buff. Grabbing a cup of coffee, catching up with friends and watching a movie are a few of my favorite things to do.

Please check out some of my work below and feel free to contact me any time.
